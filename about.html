About ArtiCYa

ArtiCYa is a non-profit organization based in Cyprus, committed to youth empowerment through European cooperation and Erasmus+ Youth initiatives.

Who we are

We are an independent youth organization working within the Erasmus+ framework to support learning, mobility and collaboration among young people and organizations.

Our mission

Our mission is to create meaningful opportunities for young people to grow, learn and engage in international experiences that promote inclusion, active citizenship and cultural understanding.

How we work

We collaborate with European partners to design and implement youth exchanges, training courses and cooperation projects under the Erasmus+ programme.

Our values

  • Youth participation and inclusion
  • Non-formal learning
  • European cooperation
